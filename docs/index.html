Moonsun Ale Co.

Developed Company Site

Moonsun Ale Company is a website that was a design by my sister, Grace Evans. You can find more of her graphic design and branding/logo work at her website itsjustgrace.com. This company is fictitious and the site was only developed so that I could practice building a live site from a mockup in a Photoshop/Illustrator file. Therefore, some of the buttons that seem to give you the ability to buy anything are not functional. This is an example of what I can help you build if you have an idea of a site that you want to create for yourself or your company.
